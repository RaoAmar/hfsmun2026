THE CONFERENCE

ABOUT HFSMUN 2026

Welcome to the Hiranandani Foundation School Model United Nations 2026. This year, we invite delegates to step into an arena of intense diplomatic strategy, critical policy-making, and global problem-solving.

HFSMUN serves as a premier platform for young leaders to engage with complex geopolitical issues, master the art of negotiation, and construct innovative frameworks for international cooperation. Our commitment is to foster an environment of rigorous academic debate and unparalleled experiential learning.

Whether you are a seasoned diplomat or a first-time delegate, HFSMUN 2026 offers a transformative experience that challenges your perspectives, sharpens your rhetorical skills, and empowers you to draft the architecture of tomorrow's global landscape.

Registration details and fees will be communicated via the official registration form. Please reach out to hfsmun@hfspowai.in for queries.

A position paper outlines your country's stance on the committee's agenda. It is highly recommended and factors into Best Delegate awards. The template is available in our Resources section.

Western or Indian formal attire is expected throughout all three days. The Code of Conduct document in our Resources section has full details.
