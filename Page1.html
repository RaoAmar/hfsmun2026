// About

New Age Diplomacy

HFSMUN 2026 represents the convergence of tradition and innovation — where classical diplomatic principles meet the demands of a rapidly shifting global order. Delegates from across the nation gather to debate, negotiate, and reshape the future.

Our theme, New Age Diplomacy, challenges delegates to think beyond conventional frameworks — to imagine diplomacy as it must exist in an era defined by technology, climate, and interconnected crises.

Three days. Six committees. One vision. The next generation of global leaders begins here, in Powai, Mumbai.

// FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend HFSMUN 2026? +
HFSMUN 2026 is open to students from grades 8 to 12 from any school. Both individual delegates and school delegations are welcome to register.
What is the registration fee? +
Registration details and fees will be communicated via the official registration form. Please reach out to hfsmun@hfspowai.in for queries.
Is prior MUN experience required? +
Not at all. HFSMUN 2026 welcomes delegates of all experience levels — from first-timers to seasoned MUNers. Our training sessions and background guides ensure every delegate is prepared.
What is a position paper and is it mandatory? +
A position paper outlines your country's stance on the committee's agenda. It is highly recommended and factors into Best Delegate awards. The template is available in our Resources section.
What is the dress code? +
Western or Indian formal attire is expected throughout all three days. The Code of Conduct document in our Resources section has full details.
Will accommodation be arranged? +
Outstation delegates can contact us for accommodation assistance. Meluha — The Fern offers world-class facilities and we will do our best to facilitate comfortable stays.
